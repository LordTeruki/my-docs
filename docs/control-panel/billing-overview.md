\# Billing Overview



All resource usage is metered and displayed in the dashboard.



\## Where to Find Billing



Click \*\*Billing\*\* in the main left menu of the dashboard.



\## What You Will See



\- Current usage and estimated costs

\- Breakdown by Compute, Object Storage, and Networking

\- Running balance (this may show as negative during beta)

\- Invoices and payment history

\- Added payment methods



\## Important Beta Billing Information



\- \*\*No actual charges\*\* will be made during the beta period.

\- Your account balance may go into the negative as you use resources — this is expected and normal.

\- Billing enforcement is turned off.

\- You will not be suspended or banned for negative balance or exceeding soft limits.

\- The metering system is active so we can test real-world usage patterns.



We recommend checking the billing page regularly to see how different actions affect usage.

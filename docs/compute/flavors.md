\# Available Flavors (Beta)



Only three flavors are available during the beta:



| Flavor  | vCPUs | RAM    | Disk   | Recommended Use |

|---------|-------|--------|--------|-----------------|

| \*\*Tiny\*\*   | 1     | 1 GB   | 20 GB  | Testing, light services |

| \*\*Small\*\*  | 2     | 2 GB   | 40 GB  | Small websites, development |

| \*\*Medium\*\* | 4     | 4 GB   | 60 GB  | Medium workloads, databases |



These are the only sizes available. Larger flavors will be added after beta.

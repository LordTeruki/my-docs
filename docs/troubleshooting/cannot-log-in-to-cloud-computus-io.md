\# Cannot Log In to beta.computus.io



\## Common Solutions



1\. \*\*Email Verification\*\*

&#x20;  - Check your spam/junk folder for the verification email

&#x20;  - Request a new verification link from the login page



2\. \*\*Password Reset\*\*

&#x20;  - Use the \*\*Forgot Password\*\* link on the login page



3\. \*\*Browser Issues\*\*

&#x20;  - Try incognito/private mode

&#x20;  - Clear cache and cookies

&#x20;  - Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)



4\. \*\*Account Not Approved\*\*

&#x20;  - Beta access may require manual approval — contact support if you signed up but can't log in

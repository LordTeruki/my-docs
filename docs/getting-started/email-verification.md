\# First Login to beta.computus.io



After verifying your email:



1\. Go to \[https://beta.computus.io](https://beta.computus.io)

2\. Log in with your email and password

3\. You will land on the main dashboard



\## Recommended First Actions After Login



1\. \*\*Create S3 Credentials\*\*

&#x20;  - Go to \*\*Access → S3/EC2 Credentials\*\*

&#x20;  - Click \*\*Create Credentials\*\*

&#x20;  - Save your \*\*Access Key\*\* and \*\*Secret Key\*\* securely



2\. \*\*Upload an SSH Public Key\*\*

&#x20;  - Go to \*\*Access → SSH Keys\*\*

&#x20;  - Click \*\*Create SSH Key\*\* and paste your public key



3\. \*\*Review Your Limits\*\*

&#x20;  - Check current usage under Billing



4\. Start testing:

&#x20;  - Create your first S3 bucket

&#x20;  - Launch your first VM (max 2 total)



\## Dashboard Tips



\- Use the left sidebar to navigate between Compute, Networking, Object Storage, etc.

\- Monitor your usage regularly in the Billing section

\- Use the Support Requests page to send feedback

You have now completed the Getting Started section!
